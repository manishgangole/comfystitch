Our Policies

Return Policy

We accept returns for readymade nighties within 7 days of delivery if unused and in original condition. Custom-stitched nighties are non-returnable due to their personalized nature, but we offer alterations if measurements are incorrect. Contact us via WhatsApp (+91 98765 43210) for assistance.

Shipping Policy

We ship across India within 5-7 business days for readymade nighties and 10-14 days for custom orders. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout (via WhatsApp or Razorpay/UPI, if available). Free shipping on orders above ₹2000. Track your order by contacting us.

Privacy Policy

Your personal information (name, phone, measurements) is used solely for order processing and communication. We do not share your data with third parties except for shipping purposes. Contact us to review or delete your data.
